    Eminem’s breakthrough into the mainstream. It was pretty much all or nothing for him at this point, and the huge success of the album, lead by the single “My Name Is”, catapulted his career forward. The album is noted for its cartoonish and overly violent lyrics as well as its sharp lyricism. It did very well critically and commercially and is considered a classic by most.
